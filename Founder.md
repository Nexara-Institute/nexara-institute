# Mohamed Salem  
## Founder & Research Director — NEXARA Institute

Mohamed Salem is an AI researcher and systems engineer and the founder of **NEXARA Institute**, an independent AI Research & Innovation Initiative focused on next-generation intelligent systems.

His work centers on the design of **adaptive, lightweight, and autonomous AI systems**, with research interests spanning continual learning, multimodal intelligence, efficient architectures, and applied artificial intelligence.

Through NEXARA Institute, he leads the development of long-term research programs integrating core AI theory, systems engineering, and real-world deployment, with particular interest in healthcare, accessibility, and low-resource environments.

Mohamed’s current flagship research program, **Project ATLAS**, investigates how unified multimodal systems can continuously learn, reason across domains, and operate efficiently under real-world constraints.

**Research Interests**

- Continual and lifelong learning  
- Multimodal and multilingual intelligence  
- Efficient and edge AI systems  
- Autonomous and self-improving systems  
- Applied AI for high-impact domains
